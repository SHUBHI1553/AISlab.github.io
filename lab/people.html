Research Interests

As a dedicated researcher in computer vision, I have innovated in image and video restoration algorithms, advancing the field through traditional and deep learning methods to solve imaging inverse problems. My vision is to establish a research group dedicated to the mathematical analysis and development of image reconstruction algorithms for both real-time and non-real-time applications.

  • Learning-based Iterative Algorithms: Developing image restoration algorithms with provable convergence guarantees.
  • Real-time and Power-efficient AI: Creating AI solutions for video restoration that are both real-time and energy-efficient.
  • Deployable Generative Models: Designing generative models for practical image and video restoration applications.
  • Accelerating Diffusion Models: Enhancing computational efficiency of diffusion models through rigorous mathematical analysis.
