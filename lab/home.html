About the Lab

The Algorithms for Imaging Sciences (AIS) Lab focuses on developing state-of-the-art algorithms for image and video enhancement, restoration, and generative modeling. Our research emphasizes theoretical rigor, real-time efficiency, and practical deployability across diverse imaging applications.
